Begin Order
This website is designed to do all the hard work of calculating your order of meat for Malafays

Instructions follow as below:
  1. Click to Begin Order
  2. Enter the amount of pounds you venison you have
  3. Click the item you would like to add, and enter the amount of that item you would like
  4. Once you finish adding the items click item review at the bottom of the item list page
  5. The Order Review page will give you directions on any last minute changes
  6. Once you click submit on Order Review, you will be given a summary of your order
  7. The order summary includes Total Price, Price per item and pounds per item
  8. After your finished you can reset if you want to complete another order
